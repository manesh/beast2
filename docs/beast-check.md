beast check

before you contribute, pause.

is it beastly enough

ask yourself:

do i love this
would i use this myself every day

does it solve a real problem i actually had

can i explain exactly what it does
no magic
no hidden behavior

does it remove work
or just add complexity

does it introduce lag
or eliminate it

is it correct
not close enough
not probably
exact

would i publicly give someone a trillion halos for this

will this still make sense years from now

final question:

is this acceptable

or is it worthy

when you solve your own problem
you solve everyone's problem
