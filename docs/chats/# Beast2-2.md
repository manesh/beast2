# Beast2  
  
## The Infinite Creation Tool  
  
Create images, video, animation, code, and worlds with generators designed for exploration.  
  
Beast2 is a desktop environment and creative platform for building and running AI generators. Instead of pressing “generate” and hoping for a good result, Beast2 lets you explore generative spaces, discover patterns, and refine generators into powerful creative instruments.  
  
Beast2 is built on a simple philosophy:  
  
beautiful means lead to beautiful ends.  
  
Creative tools should be elegant, responsive, and understandable. Generators should feel like instruments for exploration, not black boxes.  
  
---  
  
## Explore Infinite Creative Spaces  
  
Every generator represents a vast space of possible outputs.  
  
Beast2 lets you navigate these spaces instead of blindly sampling them.  
  
Generate variants.

Map parameter regions that produce great results.

## Generators, Not Pipelines  
  
Most AI tools focus on individual outputs.  
  
Beast2 focuses on generators.  
  
A generator is a reusable creative mechanism that can produce thousands of variations of an idea.  
  
Build a generator once, then explore its possibilities endlessly.  
  
Generators can produce:  
  
images    
animations    
video loops    
game assets    
text    
code    
  
---  
  
## Artifact-Driven Creation  
  
Every generation creates an artifact.  
  
Artifacts are browsed in a visual gallery where you can quickly evaluate results and branch new generations from promising outputs.  
  
Artifacts can be rated:  
  
Diamond — exceptional results    
OK — promising variations    
Trash — failed experiments  
  
Over time, Beast2 builds a map of which parameters produce the best results.  
  
The system learns from your exploration.
