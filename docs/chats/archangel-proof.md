Core axiom:

No proof. Only evidence, testimony, and judgment.

Expanded:

Nothing is ever proven.

No amount of evidence becomes proof.

A photograph is not proof.
A video is not proof.
A physical object is not proof.
A memory is not proof.
A sworn statement is not proof.
A scientific consensus is not proof.
A miracle is not proof.

All of these may be evidence. Some may be very strong evidence. Some may justify belief, action, trust, suspicion, rejection, or worship.

But none of them abolish the final uncertainty.

For archangel.my, this becomes a foundational distinction:

Word	Meaning
evidence	something presented for consideration
testimony	someone’s account of what they experienced or believe
judgment	an opinion offered after considering evidence
truth	what actually is
proof	not available to finite minds
