## claim

archangel.my can help civilization move toward a more truthful, compassionate, and cooperative future by preserving claims, counter-claims, evidence, testimony, and belief changes in public.

---

## why this matters

if people cannot remember what was said, who said it, what evidence existed, and how beliefs changed, civilization becomes easier to manipulate.

a public record makes deception harder and learning easier.

## counter-claims

### 1. this could become another biased social platform

summary: founder values, moderation choices, ranking systems, and user culture may distort the record.

response: true risk. mitigation: show authorship, preserve dissent, expose edits, allow counter-claims.

---

### 2. preserving bad claims can amplify misinformation

summary: false claims may spread if preserved without enough warning or context.

response: true risk. mitigation: counter-claims, belief voting, evidence quality labels, context panels.

---

### 3. the project may be too ambitious

summary: court + library + archive + social system may be too broad for an mvp.

response: true risk. mitigation: start with one claim page format and basic evidence/counter-evidence.
