**drift**  
  
A gradual degradation of meaning, structure, or correctness in a system over time, typically caused by incremental changes that are not fully validated.  
  
**Common causes:**  
	•	Replacing precise definitions with simplified or incomplete versions  
	•	Accepting noisy or ambiguous input as truth  
	•	Failing to compare new information against existing verified knowledge  
  
**Prevention strategies:**  
	•	Always compare new input with stored definitions  
	•	Prefer more complete and precise representations  
	•	Avoid overwriting verified entries without strong justification  
	•	Preserve uncertainty rather than forcing resolution
