## How Beast2 Works  
  
Beast2 turns generative AI into an exploration process.  
  
### 1. Run a Generator  
  
Start with a generator designed to produce a specific type of artifact.  
  
Examples include:  
  
- pixel-art character sprites    
- animation loops    
- character portraits    
- dialogue or narrative text    
- code snippets  
  
Run the generator and create multiple variants.  
  
Instead of generating one output, Beast2 encourages exploring many possibilities at once.  
  
---  
  
### 2. Find Promising Artifacts  
  
Generated outputs appear in a visual gallery.  
  
Quickly scan the results and mark artifacts based on quality:  
  
Diamond — exceptional results    
OK — promising variations    
Trash — failed outputs  
  
Over time Beast2 builds a map of which prompts and parameters produce the best results.  
  
---  
  
### 3. Explore and Refine  
  
Select a promising artifact and generate variations nearby.  
  
Small changes in prompts, seeds, or parameters can produce dramatically different results.  
  
Beast2 helps you explore those possibilities systematically.  
  
What begins as a single generation becomes a creative engine capable of producing thousands of variations.  
  
---  
  
Creation becomes exploration.  
  
Not trial and error.  
  
---  
  
## Why Beast2 Is Different  
  
Most AI tools are designed around producing individual outputs.  
  
Beast2 is designed around exploring generators.  
  
---  
  
### Artifact-First Workflow  
  
Typical AI tools focus on prompts and settings.  
  
Beast2 focuses on artifacts.  
  
You start by browsing generated outputs, selecting promising ones, and generating new variants from them.  
  
This keeps the creative process visual and intuitive.  
  
---  
  
### Generators Instead of Workflows  
  
In many tools, workflows are complicated pipelines that are difficult to reuse.  
  
Beast2 introduces generators — compact creative engines designed to produce entire families of outputs.  
  
A generator might produce:  
  
- a complete set of pixel-art sprites    
- a library of character portraits    
- variations of a game environment  
  
Generators are reusable, shareable, and designed for exploration.  
  
---  
  
### Exploration of Generative Space  
  
Most tools treat generation as a button you press repeatedly.  
  
Beast2 treats generation as a space you can explore.  
  
You can:  
  
- discover clusters of successful results    
- map parameter regions that produce diamonds    
- systematically refine generators  
  
This turns generative AI into a creative discovery process.  
  
---  
  
### Designed for Responsiveness  
  
Many AI interfaces stall or freeze while running models.  
  
Beast2 prioritizes responsiveness and fluid interaction.  
  
Creative tools should feel alive.  
  
You should be able to explore ideas without waiting for the interface to catch up.  
  
---  
  
### Share Creative Engines  
  
Instead of sharing only images or videos, Beast2 lets you share the generator itself.  
  
A generator package can contain:  
  
- generation steps    
- prompts and parameters    
- variants and artifacts    
- reproducible lineage  
  
Anyone can load the generator and continue exploring the same creative space.  
  
---  
  
### Built for Infinite Creation  
  
Generative models contain vast spaces of possible outputs.  
  
Most tools barely scratch the surface.  
  
Beast2 is designed to explore them.  
  
Not just to generate artifacts, but to discover entirely new creative possibilities.
