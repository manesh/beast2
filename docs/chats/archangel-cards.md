cards.md

cards

cards are the core building block of archangel.my.

a card is a small, reusable, linkable object that represents a claim, counter-claim, definition, person, event, source, or piece of evidence.

cards are designed to scale from tiny discussions to civilization-scale archives.

⸻

goals

* make information modular
* make evidence reusable
* make claims easy to cross-reference
* support long-term archival
* support public judgment and disagreement
* support append-only history

⸻

card types

initial planned card types:

* claim
* counterclaim
* definition
* evidence
* source
* person
* organization
* event
* category

more types may be added later.

⸻

minimum card structure

each card should contain:

* unique id
* type
* title
* short summary
* creation date
* creator
* edit history
* linked cards

optional:

* media
* sources
* tags
* related claims
* archived snapshots

⸻

judgments

users may publicly judge claims using:

* reject
* doubt
* idk
* suspect
* believe

judgments are public and historical.

users may change their judgment later.

the record preserves both.

⸻

evidence model

evidence itself is also a card.

examples:

* image
* video
* testimony
* quote
* document
* screenshot
* external link

evidence may support multiple claims simultaneously.

⸻

history

cards are append-only by default.

old versions remain accessible.

archangel.my aims to preserve:

* what changed
* who changed it
* when it changed
* according to who

⸻

philosophy

reddit optimizes for argument.

wikipedia optimizes for summary.

courts optimize for procedure.

archangel.my optimizes for public record.
