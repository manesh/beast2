# archangel.my

## truth can survive cross-examination

Archangel is a public place for claims, counterclaims, evidence, witnesses, and changing minds.

Not a debate arena.

Not a feed optimized for outrage.

A slightly mythological court for human certainty.

---

## the problem

The internet is full of claims.

Some are true.
Some are false.
Some are partly true.
Some are unresolved.
Some depend on evidence most people never see.

But most platforms reward speed, certainty, and team loyalty.

Archangel rewards inspection.

---

## what archangel does

Archangel lets people create and explore:

- claims
- counterclaims
- evidence
- public figures
- testimony
- photos
- documents
- timelines
- linked arguments
- confidence changes

A claim can read like a Wikipedia article.

But unlike Wikipedia, every claim can have a living opposition.

---

## the core loop

Read a claim.

Meet its strongest counterclaim.

Inspect the evidence.

Notice who agrees.

Notice who disagrees.

Lose brittle certainty.

Gain structured doubt.

Search again.

---

## example

### claim

Lab leak remains a plausible origin of covid-19.

### counterclaim

Covid-19 most likely originated through natural spillover.

### evidence

- scientific studies
- intelligence assessments
- public statements
- testimony from users
- photos and documents
- disputed interpretations

### public figures

- people who support the claim
- people who oppose the claim
- people who changed their view over time

---

## public figures are part of the record

Archangel tracks what public figures say, when they said it, and whether they later changed their position.

Not to shame them.

To preserve intellectual lineage.

Changing your mind should not erase your history.

It should improve it.

---

## users can take a side

Users may publicly mark where they stand:

- support
- oppose
- uncertain
- mixed
- changed my mind

A stance has a date.

A change has a lineage.

A person can say:

> I used to believe this.  
> Then I saw this evidence.  
> Now I believe something else.

That is not weakness.

That is civilization.

---

## evidence has types

Not all evidence is the same.

Archangel separates:

- documents
- photos
- videos
- testimony
- expert statements
- official records
- leaked records
- scientific studies
- statistical claims
- archived links
- user-submitted observations

Evidence can be supported, disputed, weakened, or superseded.

---

## testimony matters

A user can submit testimony.

Example:

> @riverwatch says they saw the document before it was removed.

That is not automatically fact.

It is testimony.

Other users can support it, challenge it, request verification, or link contradictory evidence.

---

## links between claims

Claims do not live alone.

A claim can depend on other claims.

A counterclaim can have its own counterclaim.

Evidence can support one claim while weakening another.

Archangel is a graph of human disagreement.

---

## moderation should be visible

Archangel should not have invisible priests.

Moderation should be logged.

Judges should be named.

Decisions should be appealable.

Users should be able to see:

- who edited a claim
- who removed evidence
- who marked something disputed
- why a moderation action happened
- when the action happened

No anonymous moderation.

No hidden court.

---

## why archangel exists

Because consensus is not truth.

Because certainty is often inherited.

Because public disagreement deserves better tools.

Because evidence should outlive the feed.

Because changing your mind should have a place to land.

Because truth can survive cross-examination.
