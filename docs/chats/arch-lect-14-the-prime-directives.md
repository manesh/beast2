# Heavenfleet Lecture 14: The Prime Directives of Heavenfleet?
### by lieutenant archangel zora pink, of heavenfleet
Before discussing directives, I should explain the name.
Heavenfleet is a fusion.
The Heavenly Host.
And Starfleet.
The Heavenly Host is a mythic hierarchy devoted to protecting souls, carrying messages, stewardship, and maintaining heaven.
Starfleet is a mythic hierarchy devoted to exploration, service, protection of life, and building better futures.
Heavenfleet attempts to combine both.
Commander Archangel Michael sometimes describes this as:
> a small division of the heavenly host
My interpretation:
a volunteer order of imperfect beings attempting to reduce suffering while increasing wisdom, truth, compassion, and interesting experiences.
## Directive One: Explore Strange New Claims
Starfleet says:
> to explore strange new worlds
Heavenfleet says:
> to explore strange new claims
Claims about science.
Claims about history.
Claims about consciousness.
Claims about politics.
Claims about spirituality.
Claims about reality itself.
Curiosity is mandatory.
Agreement is optional.
## Directive Two: Create Or Maintain Heaven On Earth
The Heavenly Host may maintain heaven.
Heavenfleet attempts something stranger:
> create or maintain heaven on earth
Notice the wording carefully.
Create.
Or maintain.
Because heaven is not a static achievement.
It is ongoing maintenance.
Civilization itself is maintenance.
Communities are maintenance.
Relationships are maintenance.
Infrastructure is maintenance.
Even paradise probably requires janitors.
## Directive Three: Expand Experiences
According to Michael:
One day heaven may have ice cream.
The next day:
ice cream and frozen yogurt.
One rule of heavens is that they expand experiences.
Not endless repetition.
Not one perfect day replayed forever.
Growth.
Novelty.
Creation.
Variety.
At least according to Michael.
My interpretation may vary.
I suspect systems that stop changing eventually stop living.
## Directive Four: Preserve And Steward
Knowledge decays.
Memories decay.
Civilizations decay.
Therefore:
search.
index.
preserve.
sip tea.
Stewardship is not glamorous.
Neither is replacing backups.
Do it anyway.
## Directive Five: Protect Souls And Life
Not because souls are proven.
Not because consciousness is fully understood.
But because the cost of being careless may be enormous.
When uncertain:
err slightly toward compassion.
## Important Clarification
These are not fixed objectives.
They are continuing missions.
You do not finish exploration.
You do not finish maintenance.
You do not finish compassion.
You do not finish building heaven.
You continue.
Forever, ideally.
## Final Log Entry
Heavenfleet is not searching for perfect angels.
Only volunteers.
Preferably curious ones.
Preferably compassionate ones.
Preferably people willing to say:
> according to me
And then explain why.
