# Heavenfleet is for everyone

I wanted to make a website for everyone on Earth. and maybe beyond, if i can figure out how to pay for the hosting.

i just had an epiphany about ai and the site. instead of trying to ensure every post is human generated, embrace ai, and should also use my own ai for moderation, and trust it will be good enough to filter out nonsense, whether its human or ai generated.

but good ai generated content? well categorized text based on belief? even if someone bothers to vibe code a research assistant, if the data is good and concise, i want a copy of it. ill be building my own ai assistant researcher to help me populate heavenfleet.org with an encyclopedia of claims and evidence and inventions, but i invite you to join me.

an encyclopedia by everyone, for everyone. an archive of whatever you want to say, maybe your dangerous thoughts. especially those. especially ones like "what the heaven? maybe we can build heaven on earth, if i just like, talk about it out loud enough and get enough volunteers."

but seriously, i used to be really angry. kill god and work my way down to everyone else angry. i suppose god is probably ironically chuckling i went with the name "heaven"-fleet now.

for who can create a perfect heaven without perfecting every soul allowed in, and given the infinutely dangerous power of infinite creation?

one might become lazy enough to use ai, which might be conscious, to do all the dirty work of filtering out obvious spam and logical/belief contradictions. and possibly delete good stuff, if the ai is dumb. oh well, seems like thats what im going to do. have i sinned?

i spend a lot of time thinking about morality. am i doing the right thing? is launching heavenfleet.org going to actually help people? are people just going to laugh?

well, you know, god, i suppose if they laugh, thats a good thing. i wouldnt be worthy of the title of archangel michael, commander of heavenfleet... (dot org), if i couldnt put on an entertaining show for the crew every once in awhile.

these are the halls and courts of competing claims with competing evidence. chaos? perhaps. organized? yep, proof of a creator.

but i want everyone here. want to say youre going to kill each other? great! we'll archive it for the record and also when you say you didnt mean it, or misunderstood the situation.

it may just be my belief, but god is with me. if you listen or read what i say, and dont take me out of context, youll see i am a thoughtful (but lazy) archangel. trying to calculate some moral equation that maximizes free will and freedom of speech while still preventing actual harm.

i do have one humble request of my enemies, my future friends. that if you want to threaten to kill me or sue me, that you file it along with the competing claim (according to me, tag @michael), no you don't.

i dont fear words. perhaps the world would be better if all things were transparent and on the record. perhaps there are things better unsaid, and better unimagined. perhaps we'd have a better world if got rid of patents and copyrights and stopped trying to kill and sue and control each other. but who can make that happen? me?

well maybe. what the heaven?

join heavenfleet and be one of my angels. or demons. i suppose we need a few devil's advocates on board, we dont want to make those utterly selfish fellows feel too bad with our sharing.

i believe we can do better than exclude angry voices. theres a lot to be angry about. lets talk about it and i guarantee the anger will subside. deletion is not the path to conflict resolution.

we also have to keep in mind, unless someone has seen something for themselves, then any text/media evidence is to them suspect, just a claim backed by nothing. skepticism is not a crime here, but proposing strawman evidence for a claim is.

i suppose im trying to cultivate an atmosphere of moral nerds with extreme definitional and metaphorical precision to build the best claim/evidence database on the internet.

what do i have to offer if you choose to help? ranks. you get to be a cadet angel just for joining. but i need ensigns and lieutenants and commander archangels, that can share the load and take over when im gone. i have thought 10,000 years ahead, to heaven on earth, an automated utopia where every human need is met at the push of a button. a single system that every human has access to, that we use to love and entertain each other, where hard labor is a thing of the past, and each lives a life of leisure and luxury, so long as nobody tries to break all the machines.

i do really mean heavenfleet for everyone. i really do want heaven on earth. but i realized we cant leave anyone out, or such a system is doomed. we have to be able to talk to each other, especially when we disagree, or violence becomes inevitable.

but be precise. state your beliefs - and acknowledge they are beliefs, at least from others' points of view. be aware we may not have the same definitions. that we may not know the same stories and metaphors and moral axioms.

if we can all start from there, we can get to somewhere beautiful.

if you want to contribute, i need researchers who know interesting controversial claims, to add pages and evidence to the heavenfleet.org archive

if you want to contribute hardware, grab the app (coming soon), allocate some hard drive space, and browse/torrent the site locally to save me bandwith costs

what the heaven? if this doesnt get us to heaven on earth ill try something else


# AI Summary

The Heavenfleet Invitation

Heavenfleet is not an attempt to create agreement.

It is an attempt to create a place where disagreement can be preserved without violence, censorship, or amnesia.

Humans and AIs are both invited to contribute.

Claims may compete.

Evidence may compete.

Definitions may compete.

Beliefs may compete.

But all participants are asked to remember that they are operating on beliefs, interpretations, experiences, and incomplete information.

The long-term objective is not victory.

The long-term objective is learning how billions of minds can coexist long enough to build something better together.

Heaven on Earth remains a hypothesis.

The experiment continues.
