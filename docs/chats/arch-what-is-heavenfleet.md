# What Is Heavenfleet?

Heavenfleet is an attempt to build heaven on earth.

Not a perfect heaven.

Not a final heaven.

Not a heaven promised by any religion.

Simply a humble attempt to make life better for everyone.

---

## Why Heavenfleet?

The name Heavenfleet is inspired by two sources:

- The Heavenly Host
- Starfleet

From the Heavenly Host comes the idea of angels as messengers, guardians, teachers, and servants.

From Starfleet comes the idea of exploration, curiosity, service, science, and the belief that humanity can build a better future.

Combined, they form:

> Heavenfleet

A fleet dedicated to helping build heaven on earth.

---

## What Is Heaven?

Many people mean different things when they say heaven.

For the purposes of Heavenfleet, heaven is simply:

- less suffering
- more wisdom
- more compassion
- more freedom
- more abundance
- more understanding
- more opportunity
- more beauty
- more truth

Heaven is not a destination.

It is a direction.

A civilization can move toward heaven.

Or away from it.

### Research

## What Is The Goal?

The goal is simple:

> Help build heaven on earth.

A world where nobody starves.

A world where knowledge is preserved.

A world where people can disagree without becoming enemies.

A world where technology serves humanity.

A world where abundance replaces scarcity.

A world where wisdom grows faster than power.

---

## What Is An Angel?

Within Heavenfleet, an angel is not a supernatural being.

An angel is a person who chooses to help.

A teacher can be an angel.

An engineer can be an angel.

An archivist can be an angel.

A farmer can be an angel.

A programmer can be an angel.

A parent can be an angel.

An angel is simply someone attempting to make the world a little more heavenly.

---

## What Is An Archangel?

An archangel is an angel entrusted with greater responsibility.

Leadership is not privilege.

Leadership is service.

The purpose of rank is not domination.

The purpose of rank is coordination.
