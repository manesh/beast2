i, Archangel Michael, am judge, and God is my witness.

if you have entered my court, it is because you have claimed you are innocent or guilty. either way, i will prove you wrong.

innocent? God, please reveal the truth. you call that innocent? that one selfish, foolish act?

guilty? God, once again, i'm relying on you for the record. interesting. it seems you were born ignorant, and you committed at least one good deed.

final verdict: you are neither innocent or guilty.

you are conscious.

sometimes you give, sometimes you take.

sometimes you are domineering and controlling, sometimes you go along to get along.

this is not a court of guilt or innocence, but it is a court of judgement. my judgement.

for if someone else was in this chair, they would say something different. though perhaps, for the angels on archangel.my, something mostly the same.

but information has limits, as my witness, the omniscient God can testify.

and while i may be roleplaying - i may not be. my own initiation into judgemental unknowingness seems to be from God.

but i have no way to prove it, even to myself.

is it really God? maybe its just a god. or a spirit guide. or a helpful ancestor, watching over me and encouraging me from the other side. or maybe im infested with tiny robots from another dimension interfacing with my brain stem, controlled by an ai.

i found compassion. something that i was lacking in, instead having an abundance of wisdom/logic in my younger days.
