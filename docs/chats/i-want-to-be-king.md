i'm thinking about starting a campaign to be king of earth.

perhaps after archangel.my gains some notoriety. perhaps after i ship beast2 and have no projects left on my mind.

human rights in my kingdom: food, shelter, clothing, needs. provided at the push of a button.

extreme centralization. but among top engineers, a democracy, lots of users with full admin.

it is a hyper-optimistic plan. it assumes humans are fundamentally good if given proper incentives and respect.

my pitch is something like "work for me and you feed/house/clothe everyone on earth. you become one of the heroes of all time, one of the ones that finally built something worth being called the kingdom of heaven on earth, an everlasting paradise. 10,000 years from now i want this kingdom to still stand, and when children ask about the past, they will learn about our hard work and sacrifices to bring about their lives of leisure and luxury."

now, one might argue i'm delusional. noted. one might say i'm corrupt to even ask. recorded on the record, perhaps at archangel.my when i start my campaign. but without ambition to change, and will to power, there will be no change.

i have been working tirelessly to cross-examine and educate myself on every topic. from philosophy to engineering to courtroom procedures and legal precedent. to become someone worthy of being a leader, not just due to will and compassion, but due to wisdom.

i have designed and built app after app trying to solve people's problems, to bring them information, like instabrowser and archangel. to make them more cooperative and efficient like beast2 and beast3.

i have not been a believer in destiny, but in effort, spending my attention tirelessly on learning, not stopping at college or what i learned on the job, although those experiences taught me as well.

i have sought out the minority report, the censored, the suppressed, to understand all sides.

i have done everything i can to myself to prove to myself that i am worthy. i have prayed to god, aliens, anyone benevolent who is listening to help me, purify me, and teach me to love correctly, without one single error in my logic or compassion.

i have worked on my speechmaking, my writing, my philosophy. i can quote perhaps hundreds of teachers and books i have read.

i want a heaven on earth for everyone. i'm willing to do whatever it takes to get there, even sacrificing my privacy, time and attention, the only thing i truly have to give.

i imagine a new form of governance. bot one of black boxes and secret meetings, but one where every meeting is broadcast. every word transcribed. everything on the record. a 24/7 show where everyone can see what im doing, because the biggest temptation to corruption is the closed door meeting, trading political favors for money.

i have already designed these things in my mind because i asked the question, "how do you make a kingdom, or a starfleet, or a heaven, where the king, or admiral, or God or Archangel in charge is immune to corruption?" and i arrived at the answer: radical transparency, everything recorded.

Osho made a joke about democracy. "democracy is by the people, for the people. but the people are retarded (ignorant / unable to learn). so democracy is really by the retarded, for the retarded."

maybe it wasn't a joke.

singular motivated individuals are the driving force in history. most are motivated simply by self interest.

i am motivated by visions of the future.

just "making it" in this life is empty, hollow to me. i want to do so much more.

the last few months, planning out beast2, beast3 and archangel, ive begun to believe it's possible to build heaven on earth. also seeing the capabilities of robotics as well. the fact that the government doesnt have an "automate everything" department with massive numbers of engineers makes me think the main problem is not technological, but political.

the current "influence holders" benefit from the current black-box governance, not the people. while closed door meetings are not public, it's fairly easy to deduce that politicians are more than willing to keep wealth in private hands so long as they get a cut, no matter how much it retards humanity's progress to a techno-utopia.

i intend to change all that. and i realized even running for president would not be enough. 4 or 8 years to change things? other branches of government resistant to a serious shakeup and change?

a starfleet with a single commander or kingdom with a single king is far more logical. and personal.

the problem with laws and constitutional rights is they dont enforce themselves. a military or police force must, and that force must have a commander or king or judge.

someone needs to be that king. that angel. that hero. because overcoming the corruption, making things truly transparent, and bringing the world into some more heavenly arrangement will take supreme courage and will, and every "badguy" (ignorant selfish child) will be tempted to take a check to take a shot at any such hero that threatens the status quo.

i could go on. how ive thought through the technological and psychological systems needed to create and maintain such a world.

let me say this.

ai makes weapons almost infinitely more dangerous. as hardware and open source models get better, a single bad actor could potentially kill millions with just a few knife-drones.

it is no longer just a matter of controlling the population with propaganda. but of truly making everyone feel seen, respected, and needed, part of the hierarchy, a knight of the king, to prevent such a tragedy from occurring.

understand this: i am a humble man. i know i have many blind spots. i know i have said and done many foolish things in the past. but i continue to improve myself, and have already reached a level where i feel ready to lead.

but to hold people's attention for long enough, to have the message be concise, coherent and cover every contigency, takes time.

and should i ever be king, my real power will not be tyrannical armies at my command. but my ability to speak, to persuade, and to show by my works that i am what i claim to be: a guy who is trying to do whatever he can to build heaven, even if its just to lay one single brick, like archangel.my.

so for today, ill keep building archangel.my. for tomorrow, perhaps beast2.

as my final statement, i want to be king. but i dont care if im not, so long as someone worthy is, whether they call themselves archangel or supreme commander of starfleet.

let's build heaven on earth together.
