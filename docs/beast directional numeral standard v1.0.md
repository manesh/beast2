the system encodes a productive cycle:

9 -> 7 -> 8 -> 9

⸻

design principles

1. fixed meaning

each numeral has one stable role

no drift

⸻

2. mechanical first

every numeral must have a usable mechanical meaning

symbolic meanings may exist
but may not replace mechanical meaning

⸻

3. symbolic resonance allowed

symbolic layers help memory and teaching

examples:

7 up = heaven
9 down = landfill
6 right = beastly and right


⸻

4. phonetic anchors matter

where possible, spoken beast forms should align with numerals

examples:

4 = fore
sev = 7
