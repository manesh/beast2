## What Is a Generator?  
  
A generator is a reusable creative engine.  
  
Instead of producing a single image, video, or piece of text, a generator produces entire families of artifacts based on adjustable inputs.  
  
Think of it like a musical instrument.  
  
A piano does not create one piece of music.    
It creates an infinite number of possible pieces depending on how it is played.  
  
A Beast2 generator works the same way.  
  
You design a generator once, and then explore the creative space it defines.  
  
Generators may include:  
  
- prompts    
- models    
- generation parameters    
- preprocessing or postprocessing steps    
- multiple generation stages  
  
Once created, a generator can produce thousands of variations of an idea.  
  
Generators are also portable.    
They can be shared as files that anyone can load into Beast2 and explore.  
  
Instead of sharing only outputs, creators can share the creative engines that produced them.

## Example: Pixel-Art Character Generator  
  
Game developers often need many variations of character sprites.  
  
Creating these by hand can take days or weeks.  
  
A Beast2 generator can automate much of this process while still allowing creative control.  
  
### Step 1 — Create Character Art  
  
The generator begins by producing character artwork using an image model.  
  
The prompt requests:  
  
- a character in a neutral pose    
- no background    
- clean silhouette    
  
This creates a base character design.  
  
---  
  
### Step 2 — Convert to Pixel Art  
  
The character image is converted into pixel-art style.  
  
A palette reduction step limits colors and produces a clean pixel-art aesthetic.  
  
This prepares the image for sprite animation.  
  
---  
  
### Step 3 — Generate Animation  
  
The generator then produces animation frames.  
  
For example:  
  
“character turns right and begins walking in place.”  
  
This produces a sequence of frames representing the motion.  
  
---  
  
### Step 4 — Create a Loop  
  
The final frames are refined into a seamless animation loop.  
  
Additional steps may include:  
  
- palette correction    
- frame alignment    
- scaling to sprite resolution    
- transparent background  
  
The result is a clean looping sprite animation.  
  
---  
  
### Step 5 — Package the Generator  
  
The entire process can be packaged as a reusable generator.  
  
The package may contain:  
  
- generation steps    
- prompts    
- parameters    
- example outputs    
- variant prompts  
  
Anyone can load the generator into Beast2 and create their own character variations.  
  
---  
  
## Why This Matters  
  
Instead of creating one character animation, the generator can produce hundreds of variations.  
  
Different characters    
Different styles    
Different motion variations  
  
Game developers can explore ideas rapidly and select the best results.  
  
A single generator can produce an entire library of game assets.
