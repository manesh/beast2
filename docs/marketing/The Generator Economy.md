**The Generator Economy**  
  
Beast2 begins as a tool for building and exploring generators.  
  
Over time, it can grow into something larger: an ecosystem where generators, artifacts, and compute form a collaborative creative economy.  
  
This vision unfolds in stages.  
  
⸻  
  
**Phase 1 — Generator Sharing**  
  
The first step is simple: creators share generators.  
  
Instead of uploading only images or videos, creators upload the generator itself.  
  
A shared generator may include:  
	•	prompts  
	•	models  
	•	generation steps  
	•	parameters  
	•	example artifacts  
  
Anyone can download the generator and continue exploring the same creative space.  
  
Creators are no longer sharing static outputs.  
  
They are sharing **creative engines**.  
  
⸻  
  
**Phase 2 — Community Compute**  
  
Generative systems require compute.  
  
Beast2 can allow users to contribute idle compute power to the network.  
  
When users contribute compute, they earn tokens.  
  
Those tokens can then be used to run generators created by other members of the community.  
  
The system becomes a cooperative network where compute resources are shared and balanced across participants.  
  
⸻  
  
**Phase 3 — Mining Diamonds**  
  
As generators run across the network, they produce artifacts.  
  
Most artifacts are discarded.  
  
A few are exceptional.  
  
Users identify these exceptional artifacts as **diamonds**.  
  
Diamonds represent valuable discoveries within generative space.  
  
These discoveries help improve generators and guide future exploration.  
  
⸻  
  
**Phase 4 — Collective Discovery**  
  
As artifacts and ratings accumulate, Beast2 can analyze patterns across many users.  
  
The system can discover:  
	•	parameter ranges that produce the best results  
	•	prompt structures that consistently work well  
	•	generators that explore interesting creative spaces  
  
The community gradually maps portions of the vast generative universe.  
  
⸻  
  
**Phase 5 — Creative Economies**  
  
Generators themselves become valuable.  
  
Creators may publish generators for others to use.  
  
Developers may build specialized generators for:  
	•	game assets  
	•	character design  
	•	animation  
	•	storytelling  
	•	procedural worlds  
  
Instead of selling static content, creators can share **generative systems** that produce entire libraries of creative work.  
  
⸻  
  
**Phase 6 — Infinite Creation**  
  
At scale, the network becomes a collaborative engine for exploring generative space.  
  
Thousands of creators experiment with generators, share discoveries, and refine creative tools.  
  
Instead of isolated experiments, the community builds a growing map of creative possibilities.  
  
The result is not just a platform for generating artifacts.  
  
It becomes a system for exploring an effectively infinite creative universe.  
  
⸻  
  
**The Long-Term Vision**  
  
Beast2 begins as a desktop tool.  
  
But over time it can evolve into a global creative network where:  
  
generators are shared  
compute is cooperative  
discoveries are collective  
  
The goal is not merely to generate more content.  
  
The goal is to build an environment where people explore generative systems together.  
  
A place where new creative worlds can emerge.  
  
⸻
